Our Mission

To instill a sense of responsibility for the environment and a personal commitment to protect and preserve the environment.

Create a carbon neutral campus (Net zero carbon emission).

Train students/faculty/staff to efficiently implement sustainability projects.

About Us

Amidst the thickly populated concrete jungles of the suburban Kurla,
of Mumbai district, DBIT is an oasis of greenery. With a huge football
ground and abundant lush green trees it's full of serenity and is a home
to a variety of birds. With our Don Bosco values, we the students, faculty
& staff of DBIT pledge to make our campus carbon neutral, create
environmental awareness and promote sustainability with sustainable lifestyle
using innovative engineering.
